In this page are hosted some charts about the population in Lowndes County, Alabama. The diagram on the left shows the distribution of age groups in Lowndes County over the last 7 years. Use the arrow keys to observe the changing population on the displayed year. The blue bars are the male population for each five-year age bracket, while the pink bars are the female population; the bars are partially transparent so that you can see how they overlap.
